FAQ

Everything you may want to know.

Short answers to the most important questions before joining.

I have no experience in dancing, will it suit me?

Absolutely! This program is created for complete beginners. Each step is explained slowly and clearly, so you'll feel confident from the very first lesson.

What if I don't have a beautiful costume or special training?

You don't need any costume or preparation. Just comfortable clothes you can move in. If you decide to dress up later - that's just a fun bonus!

How much time will the lessons take?

Each lesson is around 30-60 minutes. You can practice anytime, at your own pace.

Will I have access to the lesson recordings after I complete all the sessions?

Yes! You'll have access to all lessons for one full year, so you can repeat and practice as much as you want.

What if I change my mind / don't like it?

No worries - we have a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you feel this program isn't for you, I'll refund your payment.

What if I miss a day?

No problem! You can always come back and continue from where you left off. The program is flexible for your lifestyle.

I feel shy about dancing, what if I can't do it?

That's the beauty of online learning - you dance in your own space, no pressure, no judgment. And you'll be surprised how quickly you start to feel graceful.

What language is the program in?

All lessons are in simple English, perfectly clear for beginners. Even if English is not your native language, you'll easily understand everything. Subtitles will also be in Spanish.

Am I too old to start dancing?

Not at all! Belly dance celebrates femininity at every age. Whether you're 20 or 60, it helps you reconnect with your body, move gracefully, and feel radiant from within.

Will I really be able to dance after 10 lessons?

Yes! You'll learn step by step, and by the end you'll put everything together into a full dance choreography.
